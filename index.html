Claude Google Contacts Connector

This application allows users to securely connect their Google Contacts to Claude in order to automatically find and use contacts when composing emails.

When a user asks Claude to find a contact, the application retrieves relevant contact details such as name, email address, or phone number directly from Google Contacts.

The application operates in read-only mode and does not modify, delete, or store any contact data.

This integration improves productivity by eliminating manual contact lookup and reducing errors when drafting emails.
